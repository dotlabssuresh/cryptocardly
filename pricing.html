Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our pricing

Are there any setup fees?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans. You only pay the monthly subscription fee.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my card limit?

You'll be automatically upgraded to the next tier, or you can contact us to discuss custom pricing for higher volumes.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, we offer significant discounts for high-volume customers. Contact our sales team to discuss custom pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency payments. Enterprise customers can set up invoicing.

Is there a long-term contract required?

No long-term contracts required. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Annual plans offer 15% discount.
