Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Effective Date: August 6, 2024
Last Updated: August 6, 2024

CryptoCardly ("Company", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our platform.

1. Information We Collect

Business Information

Company name, contact person, email, phone number.

Account Credentials

Username, password (encrypted).

Usage Data

IP address, browser type, device information, access logs.

Support Data

Information you voluntarily provide when contacting support.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide access to the CryptoCardly platform.
  • For technical support and customer service.
  • To improve platform functionality and security.
  • To communicate system updates, maintenance, or service notifications.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. No Financial Data Storage

Important Notice

CryptoCardly does NOT store, process, or handle any financial data related to cardholder transactions. All payment processing, card issuance, and financial transactions are managed by third-party financial institutions and partners directly integrated by our clients.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to provide our services (e.g., hosting providers).

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be notified via email or platform notification.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

contact@cryptocardly.com
